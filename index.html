01 / About

Half academic, half operator.

I'm most at home where messy research questions collide with the discipline of shipping software people actually use.

I teach Arts and Big Data and Data Mining in SKKU's College of Software, and I run 2point5p, Inc. — a Delaware C-corp building AI-native products across finance, talent mobility, and academic publishing.

My day moves between lecture notes and pull requests: a morning class on visual analytics, an afternoon reviewing for NeurIPS, an evening debugging a trading engine. I care about how AI changes the texture of expert work — the parts we still want humans inside of, and the parts we can let go.
